Isla Margarita

Just an hour away from the capital of Caracas, Isla Margarita offers visitors amazing food, beautiful beaches and amazing nightlife. It is only a four hour ride from tip to tip, therefore, you will be able to visit a lot of it during your stay!

Salto el Angel

Known as the highest waterfall in the world, el Salto el Angel is something you definetly don't want to miss. It's situated in the southern most state of the country, bordering Brazil. Once there you will be able to see how the numerous indigenous tribes have strived for years and ge to see landscapes that have inspired numerous movies such as Up and Jurassic Park.

Los Roques

Just half an hour away from the capital of Caracas by plane, this archipelago offers the most beautiful, pristine and uninhabited beaches you'll ever get to experience. It's comprised by more than 15 small islands, most of them having no human intervention. If you like to scuba dive, fish or watersports, there is no place like Los Roques.
