Los Roques

How to get there

    By boat: There are no public transportation services offerd to the archipelago. One must charter a boat or a yatch to get to the islands as well as stay in it. Boat charterts range from US$1000 - US$2000 depending on the size of the boat.

    By plane: There are no public transportation services offerd to the archipelago. One must charter a plane in order to get to the island.Charter flights range from US$400 - US$600 roundtrip.

What to do

    Scuba diving: If you are a certified diver, this is the place to go. The most colorful coral reefs and marine life can be seen here. If you don't have your own equipment, you can rent one on the main island called El Gran Roque. Prices vary between US$50 - US$100.

    Fishing: This is the easiest and cheapest acitivity you can do in Los Roques. Since there is so much marine life, you can just use the simplest fishing gear and small bait and you will be surprised on how much you will be able to catch.

    Watersports: There are plenty of possibilities in regards to watersports. One of the most popular is kite surking. Reliable winds and shallow waters make it the perfect spot for the sport. One must bring their own equipment.
