Isla Margarita

How to get there

    By boat: A ferry can be taken in the coastal city of Catia la Mar, just an hour away from Caracas.

    By plane: From Maiquetia International Airport, daily flights are offered to the islands. Fares go from US$100 - US$200.

What to do

    Beaches: There are multiple beaches to go to. The best ones are Playa Guacuco, Playa Parguito and Playa el Agua.

    Surf: Playa Parguito has the best waves on the island. You can rent a board right there in the beach.
