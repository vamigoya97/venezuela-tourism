El Salto Angel

How to get there

    By car: It is highly recommended that if you want to get there driving, you rent your own vehicle. It is an 8 hour ride which could become dangerous if one does not go in their own car.

    By plane: Several flights a week are offered to that region. From Maiquetia Airport you must fly to Canaima Airport and then take a short bus ride to the falls. The airfares are between US$200 - US$600 depending on the season.

What to do

    Trekking: The best activity once in Canaima is trekking. There are infinite trails that you can take. The most famous one would be going up the Tepuy (rock formation from which the water falls). It takes around 2 - 3 hours and its considered an intermediate level hike.

    River slide: Another activity would be trekking through a flowing river. One can hike all the way to the start of the river where there's a cave, a swing and a waterfall one can jump into. This is considered a beginner hike.

    Helicopter ride: For the most adventorous, renting a helicopter would be your best bet. You will be able to see the waterfall from the air and the most amazing views.Helicopter rentals range from US$400 - $US600.
